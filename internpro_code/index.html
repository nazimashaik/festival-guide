Welcome to the Festival Guide

Discover vibrant cultural festivals celebrated across the world. Explore the history, activities, and traditions of famous festivals like Holi, Diwali, and more!

Festival of Colors - Holi

Holi is a joyous celebration of colors, love, and spring. It marks the triumph of good over evil and is celebrated with colors, music, and food.

Festival of Lights - Diwali

Diwali is the festival of lights, symbolizing the victory of light over darkness and good over evil. It is celebrated with lights, prayers, and family gatherings.
